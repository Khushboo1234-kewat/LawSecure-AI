Criminal Law (IPC)

What is IPC?

The Indian Penal Code (IPC) is India's main criminal code. It defines crimes such as theft, murder, assault, and prescribes punishments for each offense. Enacted in 1860, it's still the backbone of criminal law today.

Why It Matters

It helps maintain law and order by guiding police investigations, prosecutions, and court decisions. Every citizen is subject to its rules and protections.
